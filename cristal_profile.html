CV
"Maps pain-relevant cell states and circuits across species — building open, reproducible tools that make rigorous analyses accessible to all."
Research Focus

Specializes in single-cell & spatial transcriptomics of sensory systems — particularly dorsal root ganglia and oral tissues — to map pain-relevant cell states and circuits across species. Develops reproducible R/Python pipelines for integration, differential analysis, and cell-type annotation, paired with epigenomic and immune/TCR profiling to connect molecular programs to phenotype.

Particularly interested in cross-species atlases, biomarker discovery, and open, training-friendly methods that make rigorous analyses accessible to collaborators and clinicians alike.
